Cereal with Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cereal
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
  • Fresh fruits (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 cup of cereal into a bowl.
  2. Add 1 cup of milk over the cereal.
  3. Stir gently to mix.
  4. Add sugar if desired.
  5. Top with fresh fruits if you like.
  6. Enjoy your cereal with milk!
